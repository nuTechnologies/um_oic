# Cocktail Collection

## Ramos Gin Fizz
2 oz gin, 1/2 oz lime juice, 1/2 oz lemon juice, 1 oz simple syrup, 1 egg white, 3 oz club soda. Shake wie ein wilder ohne eis, dann mit eis, strain in highball glass, top mit soda.

*"Authentication barriers dissolve when cocktails foam with the persistence of a well-shaken truth."*

## Boulevardier
1.5 oz bourbon, 1 oz Campari, 1 oz sweet vermouth. Stir with ice, strain over large cube, orange peel.

*"Admin paradoxes dissolve like bitter complexity in bourbon's honest embrace."*

## Paper Plane
0.75 oz bourbon, 0.75 oz Aperol, 0.75 oz Amaro Nonino, 0.75 oz lemon juice. Shake, strain, lemon twist.

*"Admin clarity takes flight when status switches from grounded to active."*

## Bee's Knees
2 oz gin, 0.75 oz honey syrup, 0.75 oz lemon juice. Shake, strain, lemon twist.

*"Wrong ports buzz with empty promise while the right hive holds all the golden APIs."*

## Last Word
2 oz rye whiskey, 0.75 oz green Chartreuse, 0.75 oz maraschino liqueur, 0.75 oz lime juice. Shake, strain, up.

*"Last builds speak loudest when the right framework finally takes the stage."*

## Code & Tonic
2 oz gin, 0.5 oz lime juice, tonic water, cucumber. Build in glass, stir gently.

*"Hardcoded URLs are the technical debt that compounds until proper config comes to collect."*

## Aviation
2 oz gin, 0.5 oz maraschino liqueur, 0.25 oz crème de violette, 0.75 oz lemon juice. Shake, strain, lemon twist.

*"Configuration soars higher than static strings when parameters navigate through proper channels."*

## Corpse Reviver #2
1 oz gin, 1 oz Cointreau, 1 oz Lillet Blanc, 1 oz lemon juice, absinthe rinse. Shake, strain, absinthe rinse.

*"Dead login forms find new life when proper OAuth flows resurrect the authentication spirit."*

## Negroni
1 oz gin, 1 oz Campari, 1 oz sweet vermouth. Stir with ice, strain over large cube, orange peel.

*"JWT tokens flow in perfect balance when auth service, admin middleware, and Vue client harmonize in bitter-sweet authentication symphony."*

## Sazerac
2 oz rye whiskey, 1 sugar cube, 2 dashes Peychaud's bitters, absinthe rinse. Stir, strain, express lemon peel.

*"Service managers orchestrate complexity while proper endpoints find their destined ports in the grand authentication ballet."*

## Vieux Carré

*"SPA fallbacks catch lost routes like New Orleans catches wandering souls - all paths lead home to the same warm welcome."*
